About Prodigy

Readers are everywhere, so are books. As a reader, I came to realize that it is not easy to source books and obtain a readable copy. That's why I developed Prodigy to make it a one stop-shop .

I developed this project in a span of 2 weeks as my portfolio project to conclude the end of foundations curriculum of the ALX School Software Engineering Program.
