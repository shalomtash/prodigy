About Prodigy

Prodigy is a platform where one is guaranteed the chance to read as many books as possible at their convenience as long as they simply sign up for free, albeit internet is necessary. There are readers and books galore. Reading has however made me more sensitive of how demanding it is to find books and acquire a copy that is legible. For this reason, Prodigy was developed as a one-stop shop...

I created this project in a couple of weeks as my portfolio project to tie up the ALX School Software Engineering Program's end-of-foundations curriculum.
